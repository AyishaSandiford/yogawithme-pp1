Let's Get You Started

What you will need

  • Yoga Mat: Provides cushioning, support, and grip during poses and exercises.
  • Yoga Towel: Absorbs sweat and can provide grip on the mat.
  • Yoga Block (Optional): Assist in modifying poses, providing support or extension.
  • Yoga Ball: Supports balance, core strength, and flexibility exercises.
  • Support Dog (Optional): For additional support
  • Meditation Cushions or Pillow: Aid in seated meditation and for support during certain poses.
  • Yoga Straps: Help increase flexibility by extending reach and aiding in holding poses.
