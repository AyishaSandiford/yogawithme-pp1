About us

Who we are

Welcome to our space dedicated to the beautiful journey of yoga! Our founder, Emily Stuart, a single parent whose body underwent significant changes after the birth of her son, discovered the transformative power of yoga through a friend's introduction. Unsure of where to begin or how to muster motivation, she found her stride by embracing group yoga sessions, realizing the immense impact they had on her.

Through this journey, she unearthed a profound sense of unity with the universe, experiencing a tangible transformation in both body and mind. This newfound harmony led her to a place where she feels incredible—physically, mentally, and spiritually.

Determined to share this life-changing practice with others, she and her team envisioned a space where anyone and everyone could access yoga easily and freely. Thus, this website was born—a hub offering live sessions, ensuring that yoga's wealth of benefits is available to all, welcoming individuals from every walk of life.

We invite you to join us on this empowering journey toward holistic well-being. Embrace the union of mind, body, and spirit as we navigate the beautiful practice of yoga together. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned practitioner, our aim is to make yoga accessible, enjoyable, and transformative for everyone.

Our Goal

At YogaWithMe, our goal is simple yet profound: to make the life-enriching practice of yoga accessible to everyone. We believe that yoga isn't just a set of poses; it's a pathway to holistic well-being—a journey that can transform lives physically, mentally, and spiritually.

Our mission is to break down barriers and provide a welcoming space where individuals of all backgrounds, experiences, and abilities can explore and embrace yoga without limitations. Through live sessions offered free of charge, we aim to dissolve any obstacles that might hinder someone from embarking on this transformative journey.

We aspire to create a supportive community where the benefits of yoga—flexibility, strength, mindfulness, and inner peace—are within reach for all. Whether you're a curious beginner seeking guidance or an experienced practitioner looking to deepen your practice, our platform is designed to meet you wherever you are on your yoga journey.

Join us as we pave the way toward a more inclusive and harmonious world, one where yoga is a universally accessible tool for enhancing both physical health and inner serenity. Together, let's breathe, stretch, and find alignment—uniting mind, body, and spirit on the path to wellness.
